Mind a Minute

Help is available, so you can focus on the future.

If you or someone you know is going through a mental health crisis, it's important to remember that support is available.

Samaritans

Call 116 123 for free, confidential support available 24/7, whether you’re experiencing emotional distress or in a crisis.

Crisis Text Line (UK)

Text SHOUT to 85258 to connect with a trained volunteer for free, confidential support 24/7.

Mind (Mental Health Charity)

Call their Infoline at 0300 123 3393 for advice, support, or information on mental health issues. Open Monday to Friday, 9am to 6pm.

NHS 111 (England)

Call 111 or visit the NHS website for mental health support, including urgent care options and emergency mental health services. Available 24/7.
