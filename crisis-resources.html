If you or someone you know is going through a mental health crisis, it's important to remember that support is available. Samaritans can be reached at 116 123 (UK and Ireland) for free, confidential support, available 24/7. SHOUT offers text-based help by texting 85258, and Mind (mind.org.uk) provides useful resources and advice on various mental health challenges. If there’s an immediate risk to safety, it’s crucial to contact emergency services or visit the nearest hospital. Reaching out to these resources is an important step toward finding help and support.
